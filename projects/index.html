goNetScan

goNetScan is a network scanner made using the Go programming language. It enables users to scan their network and gather information about the hosts and devices connected to it. The software provides a fast and efficient way to scan networks and identify open ports, allowing users to assess the security of their network and identify potential vulnerabilities. With its customizable scanning options, GoNetScan makes it easy for users to gather information about their network. Whether used for network security assessments or simply for general network management purposes, GoNetScan is a powerful and versatile tool for anyone looking to gain insight into their network infrastructure.
